## Description / 项目简介

The cryptocurrency market doesn't lack information, it lacks credibility. KOLScore uses AI and on-chain verification to transform KOLs' historical predictions into quantifiable credibility scores, helping users quickly identify opinion leaders with genuine long-term judgment capabilities.
